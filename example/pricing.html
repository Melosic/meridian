Support

Common questions about
billing & plans

Is there a long-term commitment?
No. Our Retainer plan is month-to-month. You can pause or cancel at any time before the next billing cycle. We believe in earning your business through results, not contracts.
What does the "One-time Project" include?
It's a complete sprint to launch a campaign. We handle strategy, scripting, production, and editing for 20 assets total. It's designed to give you a library of high-performance content to use immediately.
How do you handle ad spend?
Ad spend is paid directly by you to the platforms (Meta, TikTok, etc.). We don't mark up media costs. Our fees cover the management, strategy, and creative production only.
Can I switch from Project to Retainer?
Absolutely. Many clients start with a Campaign Sprint to test our work and then move to a Retainer for ongoing growth. We'll even apply a discount to your first month if you switch within 30 days.
